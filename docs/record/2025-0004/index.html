Synopsis

Anne Elliot reconciles duty, class, and rekindled affection when Captain Wentworth returns to Bath. The Waypoint edition restores Austen’s final revisions and standardizes nineteenth-century typography for contemporary readers.

Edition notes

Typeset from the 1891 Bentley edition with orthography harmonized to British standard. Features linked endnotes and stable anchors for course adoption.
